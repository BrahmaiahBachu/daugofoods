About Us

Duago Foods Pvt Ltd, headquartered in Hyderabad, India, is a leading merchant exporter known for delivering top-tier agri-based products across global markets. We specialize in the export of premium-quality rice, fresh vegetables, coconuts, red onions, frozen buffalo meat, wheat, green millet, and yellow maize. Our commitment lies in supplying consistent quality, timely delivery, and tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of each client.

With years of industry expertise, Duago Foods Pvt Ltd has built a strong reputation for reliability and excellence in international trade. Our team of seasoned professionals works hand-in-hand with clients to ensure their expectations are met with precision and care. Every export order is handled with a deep understanding of global quality standards and logistical excellence.

At Duago Foods Pvt Ltd, we uphold the highest standards in sourcing and operations. We partner with trusted farmers and suppliers who align with our vision for quality, hygiene, and sustainability. Our infrastructure includes modern facilities equipped with advanced handling and packaging technologies to retain product freshness and integrity throughout the supply chain.

We recognize that our customers value quality above all else — and that’s why we’ve implemented rigorous quality assurance checks at every stage. From sourcing to dispatch, every product is thoroughly inspected and carefully packaged to maintain its nutritional value and shelf life.

As one of India’s trusted merchant exporters, Duago Foods Pvt Ltd is dedicated to offering exceptional products backed by dependable service. Whether it’s bulk volumes or customized orders, we’re equipped to meet global demands with unmatched efficiency.

Indian Fruits & Vegetables Exporter – Duago Foods Pvt Ltd

Duago Foods Pvt Ltd, based in Hyderabad, India, is one of the trusted names in the global trade of fresh Indian fruits and vegetables. We export a wide variety of naturally grown, premium-quality produce to countries across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Africa.

India’s fertile lands and favorable climate produce a rich bounty of seasonal crops. Leveraging this abundance, Duago Foods Pvt Ltd exports handpicked produce such as:

  • Fresh Mangoes (Alphonso, Kesar, Banganapalli)
  • Ripe Bananas & Pomegranates
  • Semi-Husked Coconuts
  • Green Chilli, Onion, and Lemon

All products are sourced directly from verified farms that follow responsible and safe agricultural practices.

Why Global Buyers Choose Duago Foods Pvt Ltd

  • Wide Range of Produce: From tropical fruits to export-grade vegetables, we offer a diverse selection to suit different market demands and culinary preferences.
  • Premium Quality Assurance: Every batch is thoroughly checked for freshness, color, size, and shelf life before packaging. We follow international grading standards to meet buyer expectations.
  • Efficient Packing & Logistics: We use climate-controlled packaging systems and reliable cold chain logistics to preserve freshness during transit.
  • Farmer Partnerships: Our long-standing partnerships with regional growers allow us to maintain consistency in quality and quantity.
  • Sustainable Practices: We actively promote organic farming and avoid harmful chemical use by educating and collaborating with local farming communities.
